Poster Session - Irish HCI Conference 2013

June 2013

An experiment was designed to test the effects of Parkinson’s Law on user’s productivity rates. Two groups were asked to complete a series of tasks - the first group was given a set time to complete the tasks with a countdown timer displayed on the user interface, while the second group was not given a set time and there was no timer was displayed on the user interface. The experiment was designed to examine the differences between the groups’ time taken to complete the tasks.

A colleague and I designed and developed the experiment, which was then presented during a poster session at the 7th Annual Irish HCI Conference Proceedings.
